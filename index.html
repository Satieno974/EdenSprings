About Us

About EdenSprings Chaplaincy Ministry

Welcome to Eden Springs SDA Church Chaplaincy, where we are dedicated to providing Christ-centered psychosocial-spiritual care and support across diverse settings. Our ministry serves communities, correctional facilities, healthcare institutions, and more, extending Christ's compassion and the Adventist message. Through our trained chaplains, we aim to meet the spiritual and emotional needs of individuals in unique and challenging environments. Join us as we embrace our mission of "Chaplains - Saved to Serve."

Read More

Our Mission

We are committed to proclaiming to all peoples the everlasting gospel in the context of the Three Angels' messages of Revelation 14:6-12. Our mission is to provide Christ-centered psychosocial-spiritual care and support across diverse settings within Nairobi East Field as we invite people to accept Jesus as their personal Savior and to unite with His church, and nurture them in preparation for His soon return.

Our Vision

In harmony with Bible prophecy and the writings of Ellen G. White, we see a time when every member will be empowered by the Holy Spirit to reach the world with the Adventist message. We envision unprecedented global growth, with every member offering compassionate care and concern as a witness to their faith.

Contact
