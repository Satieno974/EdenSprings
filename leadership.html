Our Leadership

In the EdenSprings Church document, leadership is described as the responsibility and privilege of guiding others toward spiritual growth, unity, and purpose. Church leaders are expected to embody core values of faith, integrity, compassion, and service, setting an example for others to follow. Effective leadership involves listening, inspiring, and supporting the church community while fostering a culture of inclusion, collaboration, and accountability. Leaders are called to serve selflessly, prioritize the collective well-being of the congregation, and encourage others to develop their faith and leadership abilities.
